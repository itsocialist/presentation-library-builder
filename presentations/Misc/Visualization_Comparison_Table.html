Key Differentiators

Canvas-Cinematic

Hand-coded precision data visualization with custom animations. Best for technical audiences needing detailed charts.

Cinematic-Scroll-Deck

Python-generated from markdown/JSON. Rapid creation for executive briefings and structured presentations.

RLC-AI-Visuals

Auto-scrolling technical aesthetic with particle networks. Perfect for unattended conference booth displays.

Emotional-Viz

Editorial storytelling with conceptual metaphors. Narrative-driven approach for high-stakes investor presentations.

Processing-Patterns-Viz

Meta-visualization of intelligence process. Shows HOW strategic decisions emerge through parallel processing, not just WHAT the data shows.
