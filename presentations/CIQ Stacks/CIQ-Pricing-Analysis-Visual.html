Strategic Insights
Pricing Sweet Spot
CIQ positioned between FREE (Canonical) and PREMIUM (OpenShift). $1,200-2,000/node targets price-sensitive buyers while justifying premium through compliance/performance value.
Competitive Advantage
20-30% price advantage vs. OpenShift while delivering same compliance. Stable pricing vs. Rancher's 4-9x increase creates switching opportunity.
Revenue Trajectory
Conservative projection: $75K (Y1) → $800K (Y2) → $3.8M (Y3). Matches early Rancher growth pattern (doubled ARR in 2 years).
Market Validation
Competitor success proves platform economics work ($1.8B OpenShift, $65.8M Rancher). CIQ targets underserved niches (compliance-first, HPC-optimized) they don't address well.
