CIQ PLATFORM DEPLOYMENT MODELS

Hosting, Deployment & Control Plane Architecture
Control Plane Architecture by Deployment Model
Cloud Marketplace
Self-Service | AWS, Azure, GCP
Hosting
Customer cloud account. Customer pays cloud provider for compute/storage. CIQ charges via marketplace billing.
Deployment
1-Click launch from marketplace. Pre-configured AMI/image with full CIQ Stack. Customer selects instance size and deploys.
Control Plane & UI
Customer operates via CIQ product UIs:
Ascender Pro UI: Manages base OS, non-container apps, ensures consistent security/hardening (Secure/Automation stacks)
Fuzzball UI: Orchestrates RLC-H containers as workloads (Secure stack) | Dynamic workload management (HPC stack)
Warewulf UI: Manages infrastructure and performance/hardened OS (HPC stack only)
Compliance
Customer responsible for compliance. RLC-H containers pre-hardened with STIG, CIS, PCI-DSS configurations. Customer runs audits and attestation.
Best for: Cloud-native teams, dev/test, SMB, departmental purchases
Time to deploy: Hours to days (self-service)
Managed Service
CIQ-Operated | Virtual SaaS | OpEx Model
Hosting
CIQ cloud account OR customer cloud account. CIQ operates infrastructure. Monthly subscription includes compute/storage costs.
Deployment
CIQ deploys on behalf of customer. Fully configured, tested, and hardened before handoff. Customer provides requirements, CIQ implements.
Control Plane & UI
CIQ operates control plane 24/7. Customer accesses via:
CIQ Management Portal: Compute spend analytics, quota acquisition, workload monitoring, cost optimization insights
Stack UIs (Ascender/Fuzzball/Warewulf): Available to customer for workload management, read-only for infrastructure
CIQ NOC monitors via same UIs plus internal operations dashboards.
Compliance
CIQ manages compliance posture. Proactive hardening applied continuously. STIG, CIS, PCI-DSS configurations maintained by CIQ. Customer receives compliance reports and attestation packages.
Best for: OpEx preference, risk-averse teams, fast time-to-value, compliance-heavy
Time to deploy: Days to weeks (CIQ-managed setup)
OEM Channel
Hardware + Software | Dell, HPE, Supermicro
Hosting
Customer datacenter or colocation. OEM provides hardware. CIQ Stack pre-installed. Customer owns all infrastructure.
Deployment
OEM delivers hardware with CIQ Stack pre-loaded and factory-tested. Customer activates CIQ license on delivery. Plug-and-play turnkey.
Control Plane & UI
Customer operates all UIs:
Ascender Pro UI: Infrastructure-as-Code, configuration management
Fuzzball UI: Container workload orchestration
Warewulf UI: HPC-specific infrastructure management (if HPC stack)
OEM manages hardware (BIOS, firmware, replacements). CIQ provides software control plane.
Compliance
Customer responsible. CIQ provides STIG-certified base OS and hardened containers. Ascender automates compliance scanning and remediation.
Best for: Hardware refresh cycles, OEM partnerships, turnkey solutions
Time to deploy: Weeks (hardware procurement + activation)
On-Premises
Traditional Enterprise | Full Control
Hosting
Customer datacenter (physical or virtualized). Customer owns all infrastructure. Full air-gap support. Multi-site deployments.
Deployment
Customer downloads ISOs or container images. Deploys via bare metal install, VMware, or OpenStack. Ansible playbooks for automated setup. CIQ Professional Services optional.
Control Plane & UI
Customer builds and operates full control plane using:
Ascender Pro UI: Base OS management, proactive hardening, compliance scanning
Fuzzball UI: Container orchestration, RLC-H hardened container workloads
Warewulf UI: Infrastructure provisioning, performance-tuned OS deployment (HPC)
Complete control over configuration, security policies, compliance frameworks.
Compliance
Customer full responsibility. CIQ provides: RLC-H (STIG-certified base), hardened containers (CIS, PCI-DSS ready), Ascender automation for continuous compliance enforcement.
Best for: Large enterprises, air-gapped environments, data sovereignty
Time to deploy: Months (complex procurement + implementation)
