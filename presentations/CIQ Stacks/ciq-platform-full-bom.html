CIQ Announces Cloud Platform Stack: Compliance-First Kubernetes Alternative for Regulated Industries

Reno, NV – June 15, 2026 – Ctrl IQ, Inc. (CIQ), the enterprise Linux infrastructure company founded by Greg Kurtzer, today announced the CIQ Cloud Platform Stack, a family of purpose-built container orchestration platforms designed for enterprises requiring security certifications, HPC performance, or predictable automation at scale.

The CIQ Cloud Platform Stack combines Rocky Linux from CIQ (RLC) with container runtimes and orchestration tools into four specialized configurations: Secure Stack (compliance-first), HPC Stack (GPU-optimized), Automation Stack (Ansible-native), and Hybrid Stack (multi-tenant). Each stack is available through cloud marketplaces (AWS, Azure, GCP), managed service, or on-premises deployment.

"Enterprises are trapped between expensive proprietary platforms and free-but-unsupported Kubernetes. CIQ Cloud Platform Stack delivers the compliance certifications and performance optimizations enterprises need, at transparent pricing without vendor lock-in. We're bringing the same open-source ethos that built Rocky Linux to the container platform market."

— Greg Kurtzer, CEO and Founder of CIQ

Key Differentiators

STIG-Certified Day Zero: Unlike competing platforms requiring weeks of post-deployment hardening, CIQ Secure Stack ships with DISA STIG compliance and FedRAMP packages pre-configured. Federal agencies and financial services firms can deploy audit-ready infrastructure in hours, not months.

GPU-Optimized for AI/ML: CIQ HPC Stack delivers 2-3x GPU utilization improvements over generic Kubernetes platforms through performance-tuned kernels, validated ML framework containers (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX), and HPC-native scheduling. Pharmaceutical researchers and AI labs get baremetal performance with container portability.

Predictable Pricing: Following customer backlash against recent 4-9x price increases from competitors, CIQ offers per-node pricing with multi-year rate locks. No surprise vCPU charges, no forced bundling—customers choose components or complete stacks based on their needs.

CIQ Cloud Platform Stack is available now through AWS Marketplace, with Azure and GCP listings coming in Q3 2026. Pricing starts at $1,200/node/year for Automation Stack and scales to $2,500/node/year for Hybrid Stack, depending on configuration and deployment model.
