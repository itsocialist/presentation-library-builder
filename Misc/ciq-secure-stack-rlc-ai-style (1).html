Integration Architecture Analysis

⚠ Strategic Decision Required

RKE2 Challenge: Including RKE2 directly strengthens SUSE (competitor) and undermines platform differentiation. Customer demand exists NOW, but long-term strategy requires CIQ-owned Kubernetes solution.

Recommended Path: Spectro Cloud partnership OR hardened vanilla Kubernetes with RKE2 compatibility layer.

Kubernetes Engine Options

RKE2 Competitor
SUSE/Rancher product. Meets immediate demand but feeds competitor revenue. Not strategically sound.
Vanilla K8s + Hardening Ideal
CNCF maintained, CIQ hardens/supports. Owns security narrative. RKE2-compatible.
Spectro Cloud Palette Ideal
Multi-cluster K8s management platform. Non-competitor. Full-stack orchestration with cluster profiles.
K0s (Mirantis) Consider
Lightweight distribution. Not direct OS competitor. Minimal operational overhead.
Talos Linux K8s Competitor
Immutable OS approach. Direct OS competitor. Not recommended.
