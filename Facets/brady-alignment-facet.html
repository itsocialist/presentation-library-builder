Principle: Strategic initiatives that span portfolios require explicit coordination before exec exposure.

Unified Front: Present as aligned partners, not competing perspectives

The Benefit: We amplify each other's impact instead of creating noise. Execs see a coordinated strategy team, not siloed confusion.
